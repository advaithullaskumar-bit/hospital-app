Welcome

This screen is for patients and family. You can: see your queue position, book an OP visit, and check how busy the hospital is.

  • Step 1: Choose one big button below.
  • Step 2: Read the short message on that card.
  • Step 3: If you need help, show this screen to a staff member.

Login for staff and patients

Staff can sign in to manage queue and beds. Patients approved by staff can sign in to see their OP tickets using only their phone number.

  • ✓ Staff: phone + password.
  • ✓ Patients: phone only (OTP or approval by staff).
  • ✓ Phone numbers must be registered in hospital records.
